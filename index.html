Clogged gutters, and improperly draining gutter systems, will force water from your roof to find an alternate path. The Good Shepherd team will routinely discover clogged gutters at homes and businesses are the root cause of water backing up and then causing deterioration in the roof decking, and fascia systems. Many of the most common types of roof repairs that we do can involve gutter issues. This deterioration over a prolonged time period will end up as water inside your property.
